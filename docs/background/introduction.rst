Introduction
==================

pampro has been developed at the Medical Research Council Epidemiology Unit, which is embedded as a department of the University of Cambridge School of Clinical Medicine.

It was authored by Tom White (up to and including version 0.4) for the systematic analysis of physical activity data collected in epidemiological studies.

The current version (pampro 0.5) contains adaptations made by Ella Hutchinson, at the MRC Epidemiology Unit.
